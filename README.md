# SNAIL 🐌

> **Sn-AI-l Race** — Track your 10 intentional hours with AI.

A gamified social learning tracker built for faculty participating in the *AI 101* course challenge. Log your time, reflect on what you learned, earn badges, and race your cohort to the **10 Hour Club**.

## The Challenge
Spend **10 intentional hours** with AI tools. Clock in, clock out, journal your reflections, and watch your snail speed up as you go.

## Features
- ⏱️ Clock in / clock out time tracking
- 📓 Journaling with a community feed
- 🏆 Cohort-based leaderboards
- 🎖️ Badges & milestones (streaks, weekend warrior, hour clubs)
- 👑 10 Hour Club
- 🐌 Snail mascot that speeds up as you earn badges
- 🔧 Admin panel for cohort management

## Tech Stack
- Vanilla HTML / CSS / JS (Equella-hosted)
- Google Apps Script (backend API)
- Google Sheets (database)
- Google OAuth (authentication)
